Featured Treatments

Specialized Ayurvedic therapies for comprehensive healing and wellness

Full Body Massage

A soothing full body oil application effective in enhancing blood circulation, releasing tension, relaxing tight muscles, and rejuvenating the body.

Nasyam

A specialized treatment where medicated oil or herbal extracts are applied through the nasal passage. It removes accumulated toxins, clears sinuses, relieves headaches, chest congestion, and enhances mental clarity.

Kizhi

Medicated powder kizhi and leaf kizhi (Elakizhi) are dipped in warm medicated oil and applied to the body. This treatment reduces swelling, eases joint stiffness, relieves muscular pains, and improves mobility.

Sirodhara

Warm medicated oil or kashaya is gently poured onto the forehead. This calming therapy relieves stress, anxiety, insomnia, and promotes deep relaxation and mental clarity.

Udwarthanam (Medicated Powder Massage)

The body is massaged with herbal medicated powders using upward strokes. This therapy helps in reducing excess fat, improving skin tone, stimulating circulation, and aiding weight management.

Abhyanga Massage

Traditional full-body massage using warm herbal oils for relaxation and rejuvenation. This treatment nourishes the skin, improves circulation, and promotes overall wellness.

Shirodhara

A calming therapy where warm oil is poured on the forehead to relieve stress, anxiety, and neurological disorders while promoting deep mental relaxation.

Panchakarma Detox Package

Comprehensive detoxification programs including multiple therapies designed to cleanse, rejuvenate, and restore optimal health through traditional Panchakarma methods.

Raktamokshana (Blood Letting)

A traditional blood-letting procedure to purify the blood and treat skin conditions, inflammatory disorders, and various blood-related ailments through safe, sterile methods.
